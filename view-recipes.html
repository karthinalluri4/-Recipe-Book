Famous Biryani

Ingredients: Basmati rice, Chicken, Biryani spices, Yogurt, Onions, Tomatoes, Garlic, Ginger

Preparation Steps: 1. Marinate chicken with spices and yogurt. 2. Cook onions, garlic, and ginger until golden. 3. Add marinated chicken and cook until done. 4. Layer with partially cooked basmati rice and simmer until fully cooked.
